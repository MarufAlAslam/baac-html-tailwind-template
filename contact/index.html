The Association is a not for profit organisation funded mainly through yearly membership subscriptions and occasional grants from the ACT Government. Administrative responsibility for the Association is rotated amongst community members on a financial year basis. The Association is managed by an Executive Committee elected by the members at the Annual General Meeting. The Executive Committee is responsible for the day to day management of the Association and for the implementation of the Associationâ€™s policies and programs. The Executive Committee is also responsible for the preparation of the Annual Report and the Annual Financial Statement of the Association. The Executive Committee is assisted by a number of sub-committees.
